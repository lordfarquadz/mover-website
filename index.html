How NextMove Works

1. Request Assistance

Your company contacts NextMove when additional labor support is needed.

2. Provide Job Details

Your company provides the labor rate, schedule, and job requirements.

3. Connect With Laborers

NextMove communicates the opportunity to available roster participants.

4. Complete The Job

Payment is made directly to the laborer on the day of the job through cash or Cash App.

Simple Partnership

  • Maintain an active labor roster

No upfront job payments, deposits, or long-term commitments required.

Laborer Roster Disclaimer

NextMove operates as a labor connection and coordination service. Laborers are individuals who have joined the NextMove roster and are made available to companies seeking additional moving assistance.

NextMove does not guarantee that every roster participant will meet every company's specific expectations, skill level, or work style requirements.

Moving companies are responsible for:

  • Communicating job expectations
  • Providing accurate job details and labor rates
  • Supervising work performed
  • Determining worker suitability
